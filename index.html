About Me

Hi, I'm Sakshi Ajay Agrawal — a BCA graduate from Nashik with a genuine obsession for finding the "why" behind numbers. I got into data analytics because I realized that most business problems aren't people problems or strategy problems — they're hidden inside messy spreadsheets waiting to be uncovered.

I build end-to-end analytical solutions using Excel, SQL, Power BI, and Python — from cleaning raw datasets to delivering dashboards that non-technical stakeholders can actually act on. My two portfolio projects span e-commerce sales analysis (10K+ records, Power BI) and HR attrition reporting (Excel), giving me hands-on experience with both business intelligence and workforce analytics.

I'm actively seeking Data Analyst or MIS Executive roles where I can contribute from day one. I bring strong attention to detail, a fast learning curve, and the ability to translate data into clear, decision-ready insights.
